06.FAQsSome FAQs

Here are some FAQs related to Hackman v4!!!

Who can attend Hackman v4?

The only restriction is that you must be a student! You should have a valid current university/ college/ school ID. Build your team and register.

How much is the registration fee?

The registration fees for Hackman v4 is 300 Rs. per person in the team.

How many people can I have in my team?

You can have maximum 4 members and minimum 1 member in the team!

How long is the event?

Everybody gets approx 22 hours to work on their hacks. Including inauguration and prize distributions etc, the entire event lasts about 24 hours. You are free to leave the venue before 7 PM on day one and come back next morning after 5-6 AM.
